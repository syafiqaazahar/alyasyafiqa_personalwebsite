THIS IS ME

I come from the coastal city of Kuala Terengganu and grew up in a beautiful small town Dungun in a simple and happy family. In the city of Dungun there is a very beautiful beach and because of that I like driving along the coast because it gives me peace and reduces stress. I can say this as my hobby because I often go out alone to drive anywhere I like.

Now I am studying at Universiti Teknologi Mara, Machang branch, Kelantan. I continued my studies in the field of Information Management and now I am in the final year of my diploma studies.
